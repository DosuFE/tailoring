Our Story

Crafting Excellence in African Fashion Since 2010

Our Vision

To be the leading African fashion house, known for our exceptional craftsmanship and innovative designs that celebrate our rich cultural heritage while embracing contemporary style.

Our Mission

To create timeless, high-quality garments that empower individuals to express their unique style while preserving and promoting African fashion traditions.

Our Journey

2010

Founded Enayon Sublime Design with a vision to revolutionize African fashion.

2015

Expanded our collection to include modern interpretations of traditional designs.

2020

Launched our flagship store in Badagry, Lagos State.

2023

Introduced our signature Kaftan and Agbada collections.

Heritage

Preserving and celebrating our rich African cultural heritage through contemporary fashion.

Excellence

Uncompromising commitment to quality in every stitch and detail of our garments.

Innovation

Continuously pushing boundaries to create unique and modern African fashion.
